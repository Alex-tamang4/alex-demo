Dal Bhat Tarkari is more than just a meal; it is the soul of Nepali cuisine and a daily staple for millions. This balanced platter consists of three core components: Dal (a flavorful lentil soup), Bhat (steamed rice), and Tarkari (a variety of seasonal vegetable curries). It is traditionally served with a side of Achar (spicy pickles) and sometimes a dollop of yogurt or a crisp papad.
